Intresting facts about pigeon

  • 1.Pigeons have strong muscles used for flying. They can fly at the altitude of 6000 feet.
  • 2.Pigeons can move their wings ten times per second and maintain heartbeats at the rate of 600 times per minute, during the period of 16 hours.
  • 3.Pigeons can fly at the speed of 50 to 60 miles per hour. Fastest known pigeon managed to reach speed on 92 miles per hour.
  • 4.Because of their incredible speed and endurance, pigeons are used for racing. Winner of the 400 miles long race can earn million dollars.
  • 5.Pigeons were used as mail carriers during the First and Second World War. They saved numerous lives by delivering information on the enemy's fire.
  • Pigeons are herbivores (plant-eaters). Their diet consists of seeds, fruit and various plants.
